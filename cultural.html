花は咲く | "Flowers Will Bloom"
tapping different possibilities for value

In 2015, I initiated contact with a visiting calligraphy master from Japan with an inquiry regarding a public demonstration of her crafts for a Japanese culture event I was organizing. After weeks of written and in-person negotiation with the calligrapher, community members, and the event staff, I successfully curated a unique professional calligraphy performance open to public at no charge, where the calligrapher dynamically produced calligraphy pieces with the lyrics of a live song that inspired her real-time.
